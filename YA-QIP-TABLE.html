In the past, hiring during busy periods led to new staff needing to "hit the ground running," which compromised the quality of their induction experience. To address these challenges, we aim to streamline our induction processes by reducing paperwork and incorporating a more hands-on approach - Decrease the amount of paperwork by creating engaging induction videos that cover essential information, allowing new staff to understand key processes without being inundated with documents - Shift toward a more interactive induction experience.
