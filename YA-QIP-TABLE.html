To address these challenges, we aim to streamline our induction processes by reducing paperwork and incorporating a more hands-on approach - Decrease the amount of paperwork by creating engaging induction videos that cover essential information, allowing new staff to understand key processes without being inundated with documents - Shift toward a more interactive induction experience.
